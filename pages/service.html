Our Services

Professional Land Surveying Solutions

Jambo Surveys and Engineering LTD offers comprehensive land surveying services with precision and reliability. Our team of registered professionals ensures accurate results for all your surveying needs.

01

Engineering Survey

Our engineering surveys provide essential data for infrastructure planning and construction projects. We collect accurate measurements and spatial information to ensure your engineering projects are built on a solid foundation of precise data. From roads and bridges to buildings and utilities, our engineering surveys deliver the critical information needed for successful project execution.

Learn More

Land Subdivision

Our land subdivision services help property owners efficiently divide land into smaller parcels. We handle all aspects of the subdivision process, from initial planning and design to final documentation and regulatory approval. Our team ensures compliance with local zoning laws, regulations, and environmental considerations while maximizing the value and utility of your property.

Boundary Beacon Re-establishment

When boundary markers are lost, damaged, or disputed, our beacon re-establishment services provide the solution. Using historical records, survey data, and advanced measurement techniques, we accurately locate and reinstall boundary beacons to their original positions. This service helps prevent boundary disputes and ensures property lines remain clearly defined and legally recognized.

Learn More

Due Diligence Survey

Before making significant property investments, our due diligence surveys provide comprehensive verification of property boundaries, easements, encroachments, and other potential issues. We investigate and document all aspects of a property's physical characteristics and legal status, helping buyers, sellers, and lenders make informed decisions and avoid costly surprises after transaction completion.

Sectional Property Surveys

For multi-unit buildings and complexes, our sectional property surveys define individual unit boundaries and common areas with precision. We create detailed plans and documentation required for sectional titles, condominiums, and apartment buildings. Our expertise ensures compliance with regulations governing shared property developments, facilitating clear ownership rights and effective property management.

Learn More

Topographical Surveys

Our topographical surveys create detailed 3D representations of land surfaces, capturing natural and man-made features including elevations, trees, buildings, and utilities. These comprehensive surveys provide essential information for architects, engineers, and planners working on site development, construction, and environmental projects. Using advanced technology, we deliver accurate data that forms the foundation for informed design decisions.
